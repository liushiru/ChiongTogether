Strat A Jio

If you are trying to find a study buddy, project mates or competition partners, why not start an open jio! Simply fill in some basic information, like what module you are studying or which project you are working on, and post it in our forum, then you are done! Anyone who is looking for teammates like you could approach to you by replying below your post.

Search in Forum

You can also directly search your study partner in the forum by using our filter function or typing in some keywords like module code CS2040 or project name Orbital.

Study Clique

After you find your teammates and set a meeting time, you could check your schedule in Study Clique. It not only reminds you of the time and location for your next meeting but also the work you need to prepare in advance. You can also connect it to your google calendar, so all your schedule will automatically synchronize.

Focus

Focus study mode allows you to time yourself using the countdown timer, preventing you from being distracted. White noise, soothing background picture and inspiring quotes can also motivate you to work harder.
